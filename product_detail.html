Red Turnip

Rs 369

"Red Turnip," also known as "Red Radish" or "Red Globe Radish," is a popular and colorful vegetable often found in grocery shops. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is a close relative of the white turnip. These vegetables are low in calories and rich in essential nutrients, such as vitamin C, potassium, and fiber. Vitamin C supports the immune system, while potassium is beneficial for heart health and regulating blood pressure.

Sea Food

Rs 1250

"Seafood" is a broad category of grocery shop items that includes a diverse selection of edible aquatic animals and plants sourced from oceans, seas, rivers, and lakes. Seafood is renowned for its unique flavors, nutritional value, and versatility in cooking. Seafood is an excellent source of high-quality protein and essential nutrients, including omega-3 fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals. Omega-3 fatty acids are particularly beneficial for heart health and brain function.
